I'm a DBA who evolved into an architect. After years optimizing queries in production across PostgreSQL and MongoDB, I realized the real problem isn't individual query tuning‚Äîit's **unified intelligence across multiple databases**.

Today, I'm announcing **iqtoolkit.ai** - a self-hosted, open-source database intelligence platform that brings AI-powered analysis to multi-database operations. We're currently in development with **v0.2.0-alpha launching December 2025**.

## What is iqtoolkit.ai?

iqtoolkit.ai is a comprehensive platform designed for DBAs and architects who need intelligent, automated solutions across MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and more. By leveraging your choice of AI (private Ollama or cloud OpenAI), we bring enterprise-grade intelligence to your database workflows **without compromising data privacy**.

**Critical positioning:** This is **self-hosted only**. Deploy via Docker, Kubernetes with Helm charts, or on-premises infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control. No vendor lock-in. Ever.

## Why iqtoolkit.ai?

**Multi-Database from Day One**: Unlike tools that specialize in a single database, we're building unified intelligence across MongoDB and PostgreSQL from v0.2.0-alpha.

**Self-Hosted Only**: No SaaS, no cloud hosting, no vendor lock-in. Deploy on YOUR infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control.

**Choice of AI**: Run Ollama locally (private, compliance-ready) OR use OpenAI (cloud, powerful). You decide based on your security requirements.

**Architect-Designed, DBA-Built**: Created by someone who lived the operational pain of managing fragmented database tools across multiple systems.

**Open Source**: MIT or Apache 2.0 license. Full transparency, community-driven development, and freedom to customize.
